Privacy Policy — Torah Shorts

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Torah Shorts ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app. By using Torah Shorts, you agree to the practices described below. The app is intended for users aged 13 and older.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign up using email or Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and a unique account identifier. We do not store passwords for Google Sign-In accounts; authentication is handled by Google.

Photos and camera access. With your permission, the app accesses your camera and photo library so you can take pictures or upload images within the app. These images are uploaded to our servers only when you choose to share or post them.

Usage data. We may collect basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, and in-app activity to keep the app working and troubleshoot issues.

2. Advertising

Torah Shorts displays advertisements that we manage directly. Advertisers pay us to place ads in the app. We do not use third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, or Unity Ads, and we do not share your personal information with advertisers. Ads are shown to all users the same way and are not personalized based on your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To let you upload and share photos within the app
  • To keep the app secure and functioning properly
  • To respond to support requests

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, cloud hosting and Google Sign-In), or when required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing haganahzion@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use the app at your own discretion.

7. Children

Torah Shorts is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
